## 📊 Google Ranking Recommendations

### 1. **Content Strategy**

#### Create High-Quality Blog Content:

```
Recommended Blog Topics:
- "First Home Buyer Guide Australia 2026"
- "How to Refinance Your Home Loan and Save $10,000+"
- "Investment Property Loans: Complete Guide"
- "Construction Loan Process Explained"
- "Mortgage Pre-Approval: Everything You Need to Know"
- "Best Home Loan Rates in Melbourne 2026"
- "Self-Employed Home Loans: How to Get Approved"
- "Offset Accounts vs Redraw Facilities"
```

#### Content Guidelines:

- **Length**: 1,500-2,500 words per article
- **Frequency**: 2-4 articles per month
- **Format**: How-to guides, comparisons, case studies
- **Keywords**: Natural integration, 1-2% density
- **Media**: Include images, infographics, videos

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Track These Metrics:

1. **Organic Traffic**
   - Target: 30% increase quarter-over-quarter
   - Tool: Google Analytics

2. **Keyword Rankings**
   - Target: Top 10 for primary keywords
   - Tool: Google Search Console, SEMrush

3. **Page Speed**
   - Target: <2s load time, >90 PageSpeed score
   - Tool: PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix

4. **Core Web Vitals**
   - LCP: <2.5s
   - FID: <100ms
   - CLS: <0.1

5. **Conversion Rate**
   - Target: 3-5% form submissions
   - Tool: Google Analytics Goals

6. **Backlinks**
   - Target: 10+ quality backlinks/month
   - Tool: Ahrefs, Moz
